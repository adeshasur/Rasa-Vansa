Ancient Tradition

The Spice Routes of Lanka

Sri Lanka was the jewel of ancient spice trade routes. Arab, Portuguese, and Dutch traders all came for our cinnamon, pepper, and turmeric — the original "Flavor Dynasty."

Our Farmers

Direct from Local Hands

We work directly with small-scale farmers in Matale, Kandy and Kurunegala districts — paying fair prices, zero middlemen. Their knowledge, passed down generations, is in every packet.

Tradition Meets Modern

Sun-Dried, Hand-Ground

Spices are sun-dried using traditional methods for 3–5 days, then stone-ground in small batches. No bulk factory processing — just slow, careful craft.
